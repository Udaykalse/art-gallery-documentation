# User Guide

## Overview

This guide explains how to use the Art Gallery web application. The application is designed as a single-page website where users can explore different sections through the navigation menu and interactive elements.

---

# Getting Started

1. Open the application in your web browser.
2. Wait for the homepage to load completely.
3. Use the navigation bar or scroll through the page to explore the available sections.

---

# Navigating the Application

The navigation bar provides quick access to the following sections:

- Home
- Art Types
- Gallery
- About
- Contact

Selecting a navigation link smoothly scrolls to the corresponding section.

---

# Exploring the Gallery

The **Gallery** section displays a collection of artwork.

To explore the gallery:

1. Scroll to the Gallery section.
2. Hover over an artwork.
3. View the artwork title and description displayed in the overlay.

---

# Viewing Art Types

The **Art Types** section introduces seven forms of art.

To interact with an art card:

1. Scroll to the Art Types section.
2. Hover over a card.
3. The card flips to display additional information and an image.

---

# About Section

The About section provides information about the project and displays animated statistics, including:

- Artists
- Artworks
- Exhibitions

The counters animate automatically when the section enters the viewport.

---

# Contact Form

To send a message:

1. Scroll to the Contact section.
2. Enter your name.
3. Enter your email address.
4. Type your message.
5. Click **Send Message**.

A confirmation message is displayed after the form is submitted.

> **Note:** The current implementation demonstrates client-side form behavior only. Messages are not sent to a server.

---

# Newsletter Subscription

To subscribe:

1. Enter your email address.
2. Click **Subscribe**.

A confirmation message is displayed after submission.

---

# Back to Top Button

After scrolling down the page:

1. A floating **Back to Top** button appears.
2. Click the button.
3. The page smoothly scrolls back to the top.

---

# Mobile Experience

On smaller screens:

- The navigation menu is replaced by a hamburger icon.
- Tap the icon to open or close the navigation menu.
- Select a menu item to navigate to the desired section.

---

# Troubleshooting

If an image does not appear:

- Refresh the page.
- Check your internet connection.

If animations do not work:

- Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.

If the navigation menu does not open:

- Refresh the page and try again.
