7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms are Painting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theater, Film, and Music . However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the Liberal Arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic, Rhetoric, Arithmetic, Geometry, Astronomy, and Music.

PAINTING

The art of applying pigment to a surface to create expressive imagery.

SCULPTURE

Three-dimensional artwork created by shaping materials.

LITERATURE

Written works considered to have artistic or intellectual value.

ARCHITECTURE

The art and science of designing buildings and structures.

CINEMA

The art of moving images that tell stories or document reality.

MUSIC

The art of arranging sounds in time to create composition.

THEATER

Collaborative form of performing art using live performers.
